Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Wordnook mobile game (“the App”, “we”, “us”) collects and how it is used. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Summary

Wordnook is a word puzzle game. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, or location. Your game progress is stored only on your device. Like most free games, we show ads through Google AdMob, which uses a device advertising identifier.

Information we collect

1. Gameplay data (stored on your device only). Your level progress, coins, stars, and settings are saved locally on your device. This data stays on your device, is not sent to us, and is removed when you uninstall the App.

2. Advertising data (Google AdMob). We use Google AdMob to show ads (rewarded videos and occasional interstitials). AdMob and its partners may collect and process data such as a device advertising identifier, general device information, and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads, and to help prevent fraud. See Google’s policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

We do not collect or transmit any other personal information, and we do not operate any account, login, or online leaderboard.

How we use information

  • To run the game (save your progress locally on your device).
  • To display advertising that keeps the App free.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — advertising. support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

Your choices

  • Ads / advertising ID: You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).
  • Local data: Uninstalling the App removes all data stored on your device.

Children’s privacy

Wordnook is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
